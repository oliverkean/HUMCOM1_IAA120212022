Harry Potter and the Chambers of Secrets

by:J.k Rowling

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets is a Fantasy and Adventure genre book.
It is my favorite book because I like books that is more on Fantasy, and it is the first book
that I bought for myself. The story was very interesting because of the twist of the book especially when they revealed that
Voldemort was a former student in Hogwarts. The book also gave me wonderful lessons, on how we treat people, and treat everyone equally.
I will suggest this to people who are interested in fantasy, imagination, and adventure books. It is a very nice book, and it will be entertaining if you will read all seven books.
