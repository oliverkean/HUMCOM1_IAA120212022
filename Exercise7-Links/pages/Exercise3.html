How to cook rice

In the Philippines, every breakfast, lunch, and dinner there is always rice present.
Eating a meal without a rice feels incomplete for our stomach in a day.
When I was kid my mother and lola taught me how to cook rice, and know I am the one cooking the rice for dinner.

What you will need:

  • Rice
  • Pot, or the pot from the Rice cooker
  • Water
  • Rice Cooker

Procedure:

  1. Prepare the pot and the rice.
  2. Put 2 cups of rice in the pot
  3. Use the water, and wash the rice in the pot for about 2-3 times.
  4. Put another water, don't put too much
  5. Measure the level of the water is the same as the level of your rice.
  6. If done measuring. Dry the pot with a cloth, wipe the bottom and the side of the pot.
  7. Put it in the Rice Cooker, plug it in the socket.
  8. Set it into Cook mode.
  9. Wait for 30-35 minutes. It will go in the Warm mode
  10. It is done
